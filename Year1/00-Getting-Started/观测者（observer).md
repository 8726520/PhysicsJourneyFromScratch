测量系统的分类与应用领域

科学实验：追求高精度和高准确度，是发现自然规律的基础（如测量光速、基本电荷）。

工程技术：保证产品质量、系统安全和互换性的关键（如机械零件加工尺寸测量、桥梁应力应变测量）。

质量控制与检验：在生产过程中监控产品是否符合标准。

日常生活：满足日常定量需求（如称重、计时、测体温、汽车仪表盘显示车速油耗）。

商业贸易：保证公平交易的基础（如市场电子秤、电表、水表）。

医疗诊断：提供关键生理参数（如血压、心率、血液成分分析）。

Classification and Application Fields of Measurement Systems
Scientific Experiments: Pursue high precision and accuracy, serving as the foundation for discovering natural laws (e.g., measuring the speed of light, elementary charge).
Engineering Technology: A key factor in ensuring product quality, system safety, and interchangeability (e.g., measuring the machining dimensions of mechanical parts, measuring the stress and strain of bridges).
Quality Control and Inspection: Monitor whether products meet standards during the production process.
Daily Life: Meet daily quantitative needs (e.g., weighing, timing, measuring body temperature, displaying vehicle speed and fuel consumption on car dashboards).
Commercial Trade: Lay the foundation for fair transactions (e.g., electronic scales in markets, electricity meters, water meters).
Medical Diagnosis: Provide key physiological parameters (e.g., blood pressure, heart rate, blood component analysis).
