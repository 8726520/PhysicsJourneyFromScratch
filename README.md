# PhysicsJourneyFromScratch
Description: A retired person's journey to relearn physics from scratch | 退休物理探索之旅    Public（公开）
A systematic study record of a retired individual learning physics from scratch, including experimental reproductions, thought notes, and exploratory research. This project follows a 5-year plan to rebuild physics understanding from fundamental concepts.

**中文**: 一位退休人士从零开始系统学习物理的记录，包括实验复现、思考笔记和探索性研究。本项目遵循五年计划，从基础概念开始重建物理认知。

## Author | 作者

**Retired Physics Explorer** - A curious mind from China  
**退休物理探索者** - 来自中国的好奇心灵
## Goals | 目标

### 1. Systematic Learning | 系统学习
From middle school physics to modern concepts, without skipping fundamentals.  
从初中物理到现代概念，不跳过任何基础。

### 2. Experimental Verification | 实验验证
Reproduce key historical experiments with a home laboratory setup.  
用家庭实验室复现关键历史实验。

### 3. Critical Thinking | 批判思考
Question assumptions, explore alternative interpretations, document doubts.  
质疑假设，探索替代解释，记录疑问。

### 4. Open Sharing | 开放分享
All notes, data, and mistakes are public for discussion and improvement.  
所有笔记、数据甚至错误都公开，供讨论和改进。
## Learning Plan | 学习计划

| Year 年份 | Focus 重点 | Status 状态 |
|-----------|------------|-------------|
| **2026-2027** | Foundations: Measurement, Time, Space, Mechanics | 🟢 In Progress 进行中 |
| 2027-2028 | Electromagnetism and Fields | ⚪ Planned 计划中 |
| 2028-2029 | Thermodynamics and Statistical Physics | ⚪ Planned 计划中 |
| 2029-2030 | Quantum Concepts and Relativity | ⚪ Planned 计划中 |
| 2030-2031 | Integration and Original Exploration | ⚪ Planned 计划中 |

## Current Focus | 当前重点

### Week 1: The Nature of Time | 第一周：时间的本质
- Experiments comparing mechanical, biological, and fluid timekeeping
- Philosophical questions: What is time? How do we measure it?
- 实验比较机械、生物和流体计时方法
- 哲学问题：时间是什么？我们如何测量时间？

### Planned Experiments | 计划实验
1. Temperature effects on time measurement devices
2. Material dependence of inertial mass (preliminary exploration)
3. Perception of time under different mental states
4. 温度对计时设备的影响实验
5. 惯性质量的材料依赖性（初步探索）
6. 不同心理状态下的时间感知
